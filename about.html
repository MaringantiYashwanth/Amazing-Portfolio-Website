About Me

I'm Yashwanth Maringanti — a passionate software developer and lifelong learner based in India. I specialize in building full-stack web applications with a particular interest in Java, Spring Boot, and React. I also enjoy working with databases like MongoDB and PostgreSQL, and exploring backend architectures and microservices.

Outside of coding, I love teaching, writing, and contributing to the developer community. Whether it’s through tutorials, open-source projects, or building production-grade tools, my goal is always to learn and share.
